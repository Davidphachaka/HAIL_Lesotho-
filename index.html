About HAIL

The Hub for Artificial Intelligence Lesotho (HAIL) is dedicated to creating AI awareness, educating communities, and enabling innovation that improves lives in Lesotho and beyond.

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making.
